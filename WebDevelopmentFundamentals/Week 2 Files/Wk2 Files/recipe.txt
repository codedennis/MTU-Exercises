Lunchbox Couscous Salad
A hearty couscous salad that is simple to make.  
Pack in your lunch box and you're ready to go!

Ingredients
225g/8oz couscous
1 bunch fresh parsley, chopped
1 bunch fresh coriander, chopped
1 red onion, very finely chopped
1 lemon, zest and juice
half cucumber, de-seeded and finely diced
30g/1oz sunflower seeds, toasted
4 tbsp olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper
	
Preparation method
Cover the couscous with twice its volume of hot chicken or veg stock and leave to soak for 10 minutes. Fork through and mix together with the remaining ingredients.  Leave to stand for 30 minutes. Serve at room temperature.
